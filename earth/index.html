Our Planet
Earth

The planet Earth is only a tiny part of the universe, but it's the only place where human beings can live. Today, our planet is in serious danger. Acid rains, global warming, air and water pollution, overpopulation are the problems that threaten human life on Earth.

Water

Water is vital to life, and we cannot live if there is no water. The Earth has water bodies such as lakes, streams, rivers, seas and oceans. While the water of the seas and the oceans is saline, and is not fit for consumption, the water of rivers and streams and lakes can be consumed. Water must be pure and fit to be consumed. Water is needed by man not only for drinking, but for cooking his food, and maintaining personal hygiene and cleanliness of his surroundings too. All marine species need water bodies to live in.

Animals

The Earth is home to the millions of life forms that inhabit it. There are both terrestrial creatures that live on the land, and aquatic creatures that live in water. Some life forms have become extinct, and many life forms are endangered.

Air

Trees provide us ample benefits. Trees use up carbon-dioxide from the air for carrying out the process of photosynthesis, and give out oxygen, which we need for breathing. Trees are also food for man and other herbivorous animals. Trees and forests are also home to many wildlife species. Forests help in maintaining the hydrological cycle too. They help in maintaining the water table level. They also help in keeping the monsoon systems going.

How can we save our green EARTH?

The Earth and its resources can be protected by man. Every little effort on our part to save the Earth and its environment can make a difference. Plastics, for instance, are a source of major environmental degradation. We can take little steps like avoiding the use of plastics. For example, we can avoid using plastic water bottles. We can instead use steel, glass or clay bottles to carry or store water.

Similarly, we can plant trees in our neighbourhood. Trees play a major role in ecological balance. By planting trees we can improve our environment.

We must also save water which is a precious natural resource. We must also avoid polluting water bodies. We must be judicious in the use of all natural resources. We can thus save the Earth.

We should save EARTH!
